Who is Chris Harrison?

Chris Harrison is an American television and game show host best known for his time as the host of ABC’s reality dating show “The Bachelor” from 2002 to 2021.

Harrison, who was born on July 26, 1971, in Dallas, Texas, played an important role in the Bachelor franchise, hosting spin-offs such as “The Bachelorette,” “Bachelor Pad,” “Bachelor in Paradise,” and others.

He also presented the syndicated version of “Who Wants to Be a Millionaire” from 2015 to 2019. Harrison became a beloved personality in the world of reality television due to his charismatic and friendly on-screen appearance.

During his time as “The Bachelor” host, he guided contestants on their quest for love, navigated unexpected twists and turns, and gave viewers emotional and frequently dramatic moments.

Despite departing “The Bachelor” in 2021, Chris Harrison is still a well-known and powerful figure in the entertainment world, well recognized for his contributions to famous dating reality shows and game shows.
