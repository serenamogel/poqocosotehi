About

UCSB stands for the University of California, Santa Barbara. It is one of the ten campuses of the University of California system and is located in the city of Santa Barbara, California. UCSB is known for its strong programs in science, engineering, and social sciences, as well as its beautiful beachside location. The campus enrolls over 25,000 undergraduate and graduate students and is home to many research centers and institutes.
