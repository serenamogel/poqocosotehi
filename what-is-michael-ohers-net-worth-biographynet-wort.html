What is Michael Oher’s Net Worth: Biography,Net Worth & more- Michael Oher, a former American football offensive tackle, played for the Baltimore Ravens, Tennessee Titans, and Carolina Panthers.

Who is Michael Oher?

Born in Memphis, Tennessee, he had a difficult childhood, with alcoholism and drug addiction issues. Adopted by wealthy parents, he attended Briarcrest Christian School and played college football at the University of Mississippi.

His life story was featured in Michael Lewis’ book, The Blind Side: Evolution of a Game, and the 2009 film The Blind Side, starring Sandra Bullock. Oher married Tiffany Roy in November 2022 and has a good relationship with his biological mother, Denise Oher, who has overcome addiction problems.
