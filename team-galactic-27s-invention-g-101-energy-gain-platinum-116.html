Team Galactic's Invention G-101 Energy Gain (Japanese: ギンガ団の発明G-101 エナジーゲイン Team Galactic's Invention G-101 Energy Gain) is a Pokémon Tool card. It is part of the Platinum expansion.

Card text

Attach Team Galactic's Invention G-101 Energy Gain to 1 of your Pokémon SP that doesn't already have a Pokémon Tool attached to it. If that Pokémon is Knocked Out, discard this card. When the Pokémon this card is attached to is no longer a Pokémon SP, discard this card.
As long as Team Galactic's Invention G-101 Energy Gain is attached to a Pokémon, the attack cost of that Pokémon's attacks is less.
