Father Joe And Mother Stephanie

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Wednesday, July 31, 2024

Thomas Milic is a goalie for the Western Hockey League team Seattle Thunderbirds, based in Kent, Washington, United States. What is his parents’ nationality? Let’s find out.

The Thunderbirds picked Milic as the 51st overall pick in the 2018 Bantam Draft. He has been playing for the Kent-based team ever since.

Despite being eligible for the NHL Draft in the previous two seasons, Milic was undrafted. He is ineligible for the 2023 Draft as he crosses 20 years of age in April 2023.

Nevertheless, Milic was a crucial part of the gold medal-winning Canada men’s national junior ice hockey team.

The team won the 2023 World Junior Ice Hockey Championships held at Halifax, Nova Scotia and Moncton, New Brunswick, Canada.

In the final match between Canada and Czechia, Milic posted 24 saves after performing a 43-save match in Canada’s semifinal win over the United States.

Thomas Milic Parents Nationality: Where Are They From?

Thomas Milic’s parents are Canadian by nationality and currently residing in Coquitlam, British Columbia. 

His father, Joe Milic, is a vice president at National Bank Financial, whereas his mother, Stephanie Milic, is a housewife.

Milic was born to his parents on April 14, 2003, in New Westminster, British Columbia. 

Though Thomas plays hockey in the US, his parents, Joe and Stephanie, are Canadian nationals in Coquitlam, British Columbia.

Joe and Stephanie often visit their son at his matches and cheer for him. With the blessings of his parents, Thomas has been able to give top-notch performances in his games.

Meet Thomas Milic Father Joe Milic

Thomas Milic’s father, Joe Milic, is a financier with over 25 years of experience in banking and financial firms.

Joe is currently a portfolio manager and vice president at National Bank Financial. He helps clients achieve their long-term financial goals. 

According to his LinkedIn, Milic runs the Joe Milic Wealth Management Group, which works on portfolio management, financial planning, and tax planning.

The firm also helps clients with estate planning, insurance, and banking solutions. Joe possesses a solid knowledge base in the field and has been a valued resource for his clients.

Before joining NBF, Joe was a portfolio manager and vice president at HSBC Securities. He joined HSBC in 1996 as Manager of Investment Services. 

Milic studied at Queen Elizabeth Secondary School and graduated with a Bachelor’s in Commerce in Urban Land Economics in 1993. 

He is also an FCSI fellow at the Canadian Securities Institute (CSI) and a Certified Financial Planner at the Financial Planning Standard Council.

Joe is a proud father of Thomas Milic. After the Seattle Thunderbirds signed Thomas, Joe expressed his happiness and pride that a great WHL team had selected his son.

 Thomas Milic Mother, Stephanie, And Sister Kristen

Thomas Milic’s mother, Stephanie Milic, is a housewife. She is her son’s biggest supporter and often cheers for him in his matches.

However, there is not much information available about Stephanie as she prefers not to come out in the limelight.

Milic’s older sister, Kristen, recently graduated with a BBA from the Beedie School of Business at Simon Frazer University.

She is a certified swim coach who has coached children at Brent Hayden Swim Camps and the Special Olympics.

According to her LinkedIn, Kristen is the founder of Lifesaver and Labs Batch 8. She has had experience with companies such as Spare, Blonyx, and National Bank Financial.
