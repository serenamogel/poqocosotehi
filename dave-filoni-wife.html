Dave Filoni Wife: Is Dave Filoni Married?: David Filoni was born in Mount Lebanon Township, Pennsylvania on June 7, 1974.

He attended and graduated from Mt. Lebanon High School in 1992 and Edinboro University of Pennsylvania in 1996.

At this time of writing (Wednesday, 6 September 2023), Filoni is an American director, producer, screenwriter, and voice actor.

He works for Lucasfilm, an American film and television production company founded by filmmaker, George Lucas in 1971.

He has worked on several projects including; Avatar: The Last Airbender, The Mandalorian, and on the theatrical film and television series Star Wars: The Clone Wars.

Filoni is also the creator and an executive producer of Star Wars Rebels for all four seasons and served as its supervising director for all but the third season.

He made his live-action acting debut in The Mandalorian as an X-Wing pilot named Trapper Wolf in Chapter 6: The Prisoner.

Filoni later reprised the above-mentioned character in Chapter 10: The Passenger and in Chapter 21: The Pirate.

His other film credit includes; The Mandalorian, Tales of the Jedi, American Dragon: Jake Long, Dave the Barbarian, Fillmore, Kim Possible, Teamo Supremo, The Oblongs, Mission Hill, and King of the Hill, among others.

In mid-2020, Lucasfilm quietly promoted Filoni as executive producer and executive creative director for the studio.

His promotion was not announced to the public until Lucasfilm updated its list of its executives on its website with the addition of Filoni in May 2021.
