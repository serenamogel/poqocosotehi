Legend Box (Japanese: レジェンドボックス Legend Box) is a Trainer card (Item card in Japan). It was first released as part of the Undaunted expansion.

Card text

Reveal the top 10 cards of your deck. If you reveal both halves of a Pokémon LEGEND, put those cards onto your Bench and attach all revealed Energy cards to that Pokémon LEGEND. Shuffle the other cards back into your deck. (You can play only 1 Pokémon LEGEND in this way.)

Release information

This card was released in the English Undaunted expansion, originating from the Japanese Reviving Legends expansion. In Japan, a Cosmos Holofoil L-P Promotional print of this card was awarded as a prize to participants who won at least 3 battles in the Reviving Legends Release Commemoration events held in January 2010. This print has the Pokémon Card Game LEGEND logo in the top right of the illustration box and the Reviving Legends logo on the bottom left.
