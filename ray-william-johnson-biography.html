Ray William Johnson Bio (Wiki)

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Thursday, August 8, 2024

Who is Ray William Johnson?

Ray William Johnson is an eminent YouTuber. His self-titled channel has over 9.62 million subscribers with nearly 3 billion views in 202020. His channel was one of the most viewed channels on YouTube in 2016.

He is not only a YouTuber but also an actor, comedian, writer, director, rapper, songwriter, and singer as well.

What are the birth facts of Ray William Johnson?

Ray was born on 14 August 1981, in Oklahoma City, Oklahoma, USA. The identities of his father and mother are unknown.

His nationality is American and his ethnicity is mixed (English, German, Scottish).

Ray William Johnson had no intention to be an actor. He was brought up in a middle-class family with his mother as a nurse.

Though he had no interest in the acting field, he is now a well-known YouTuber with a decent number of fan followings.

Education

Ray William Johnson graduated in 1999 from Norman North High School as an average student. But, he was very active in extracurricular activities along with sports like lacrosse, football, and hockey.

For graduation, he joined Columbia University for the purpose of majoring in Law. However, he was not able to major in law and went for History as his major.

From that time he started to publish some videos on YouTube, and his followers started to increase. This made him more active to publish videos and make good earnings.

Ray William Johnson: Professional Life, Career

In April 2009, Ray William Johnson posted his first video on his YouTube channel ‘Ray William Johnson’ and initiated his career as a YouTuber.

Before starting his YouTube channel he ran a channel ‘viral video’, having more than 10.5 million subscribers, and was the first channel to cross 5 million subscribers in 2011.

‘The equal Three’ series went to get immense success but, due to some problems with the Makers Studio, he established his own Studio ‘Equals Three Studios’ in November 2012.

In December 2013, he announced that he will be leaving ‘ The Equals Three’ and, will be joining his other projects, mainly his YouTube channel ‘Ray William Johnson.

Even after his immense success with ‘The Equals Three’, he made some of the best comedy videos and uploaded them to his new YouTube channel ‘Ray William Johnson. Now, he has over 10 million subscribers and over 3 billion views on his recent channel.

Apart from his career as a YouTuber, he is also an actor who already worked for some series like Riley Rewind and some movies like ‘We Love You, ‘Who’s Driving Doug?’ and some others.
