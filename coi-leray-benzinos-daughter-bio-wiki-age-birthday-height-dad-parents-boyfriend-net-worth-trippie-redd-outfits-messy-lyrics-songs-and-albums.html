Coi Leray Songs and Albums: Coi Leray Wiki

Known for her aggressive delivery, Coi seamlessly combines rap and singing. She can be heard spitting fire bars over skittering beats accompanied by heavy bass. Her songwriting game is also on point, providing substance in the messages she delivers.

“G.A.N”, Coi’s first track to do numbers were released in December 2017. She followed closely with another head-turner “Pac Girl.” The official videos for G.A.N and Pac Girl were released in January and May 2018 respectively. They were directed and edited by Unique.

Coi’s debut mixtape, EverythingcoZ, was released in March 2018. It produced additional singles “No Letting Up”, “GoldRush” and “Get It” featuring Justin Love. Guest appearances were made by Sule, Gu Mitch, and MartianOnTheBeat.

The non-album single “No Longer Mine” was released in September 2018 by VFiles, LLC. Coi closed out 2018 with “Huddy”, released by Republic Records. The track didn’t disappoint, racking up 370K+ spins on SoundCloud in 4 months. The official music video attracted another 1.6+ million views in the same time frame.

In addition to her solo effort, Coi has collaborated with a number of other hip-hop artists. She appeared on the singles “Games” by K Dos, and “Come Home” with Tatted Swerve. The Jersey emcee performed alongside Trippie Redd on Redd’s 2019 Life’s A Trip Tour. The nationwide tour will run from January 26 in Sauget, Illinois, to February 28 in Seattle, Washington.
