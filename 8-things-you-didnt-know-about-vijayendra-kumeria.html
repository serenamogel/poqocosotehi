8 Things You Didnt Know About Vijayendra Kumeria

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Thursday, August 15, 2024

➡ Vijayendra Kumeria Biography

Vijayendra Kumeria was born on 12 October 1986. He is a well-known and well-established face of Indian television. The aspiring and phenomenal actor is most popularly known for his role as Suraj Rajvanshi and Raghav in Colors TV’s Udaan Sapnon Ki. He has roots in Gujrat means he is a Gujarati and his hometown is Ahmedabad. His wife’s name is Preeti Bhatia, who works in the airlines. It would be quite interesting that he had to convince his parents in order to work in the glamorous world of showbiz. Kumeria quit his civil aviation job after working for years to pursue acting, which was his dreamwork since childhood. Initially, he faced numerous rejections while doing rounds in production houses in Mumbai. In 2011, he bagged the role of Jolly Bhardwaj in Chotti Bahu, making his television debut and it brought him a lot of name and fame. Here we are telling you 8 interesting facts about him that you will be surprised to know and amazed to not know them before!

8 Things You Didn’t Know About Vijayendra Kumeria

1- From Aviation to Acting

Maybe this amazing fact has been hidden from you till now that he, before stepping into the glamorous world of the Film industry, worked for the airline. He had an excellent position there along with the best pay. But the dreams took him into the world of acting and he then quit his aviation job.

2- His Debut Project

Offcourse you all know him very well now. But do you about the debut project of this very fine actor? If no, then you do not have to worry about it as we have all the information about it! He did his TV Debut with  Chotti Bahu which was aired from 2011 to 2012. This serial brought him a lot of recognition.

3- A Binge-Watcher

Maybe you do not know about this hidden fact about him that he is crazy for the films and shows. He is an active viewer of them. If we talk about his Favorite Films then these are, We Are Your Friends (Hollywood, 2015) and Up in the Air (Hollywood, 2009). While his Favorite TV Show is The Simpsons (American, 1989 to present). Interesting! No?

4- His Biggest Wish

Maybe you are considering him as your favorite celebrity but do you know the biggest wish of your favorite celebrity? If no, then you do not have to worry as we are telling you his secret biggest wish! He wishes to write scripts. Let’s see when his wish will come true. We sincerely hope that it will come true soon!

5- His Personal Life

It must be quite an interesting fact about which we are quite sure that you do not know before that he worked in the airline as a cabin crew and he met his wife to be there. He along with his wife Preeti Bhatia, both worked together at Jet Airways and from there they developed understanding and later got married. The couple has not had any children yet.

6- Nickname

We are sure that it might be an interesting fact to b known about him that he has got a nickname too. Actually, just spell his name yourself and see it is somehow difficult to use in the routine! So his nickname that is super easy to be used instead of his full name is Vijay. Easy?

7- A True Libra

He was born on the 12th of October. In this way his zodiac sign is Libra and interestingly, he has got all the qualities of his star. He is Diplomatic, Fair has a strong sense of justice, somehow idealistic, always prefers to look on the bright side, social, clever, indecisive, and non-confrontational.

8- Non-controversial

The best quality of him among many is that he is 0% controversial. He hates controversies as he believes firmly in fair play. He never does cheap tactics to bring the attention of media and fans in a negative manner. In this way, he is truly a gem.
