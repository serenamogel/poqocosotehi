Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Wednesday, January 24, 2024

Kubota MX5000SU Price, Reviews, Specs, Engine Oil Capacity, Weight, Serial Numbers, Attachments, Features & Pictures

Kubota MX5000SU Utility Tractor is equipped with a Kubota V2403 diesel 4-cylinder liquid-cooled engine, which delivers 52.2 hp (38.9 kW) power at a rated rpm of 2700.

Kubota MX5000SU Utility Tractor featured a Kubota 2.4L 4-cyl diesel engine, 4×2 2WD, hydrostatic power steering, mechanical wet disc brakes, 8-speed partially synchronized transmission, 2,866 lbs Rear lift (at ends), 3,285 lbs weight, 8 qts 7.6 L engine oil capacity, 8 forward and 4 reverse gears.

Kubota MX5000SU Utility tractor attachments include Kubota LB702 Loader.
