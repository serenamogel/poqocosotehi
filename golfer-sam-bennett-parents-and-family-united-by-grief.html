Golfer Sam Bennett Parents and Family United By Grief

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Friday, August 16, 2024

Golfer Sam Bennett dad Mark Leon Bennett was a dentist from Madisonville Texas. Sam grew up with his two brothers Marcus and Jake Bennett.

His intrigue with golf was sparked by his father and siblings, who were all involved in sports. His father, Mark, taught Sam and his two brothers to play golf on a public nine-hole course in their hometown.

The 23-year-old has been playing the game since he was young. He is the current reigning U.S. Amateur champion and a former World No. 1. Bennett is currently ranked sixth globally.

Furthermore, Bennett will be competing at Augusta National Golf Course as part of the 2023 Masters’ Augusta amateurs. Gordon Sargent, Ben Carr and Harrison Crowe will join him in the competition, which begins on April 6, 2023.

Sam Bennett Parents

Sam Bennett parents are Mark Leon Bennett and Stacy Sean Bennett. Sam’s father passed away In 2021 after a long battle with Alzheimers

Mark and Stacy married in a private ceremony on 19 June 1993.

On their 24th anniversary, Stacy posted a picture of them enjoying the snowy weather and a caption about the highs and lows they have faced together.

She also mentioned that they had overcome many challenges and celebrated their achievements. Mark and Stacy may have known each other since childhood, as they graduated from Madisonville High School.

Stacy frequently posts pictures on her Facebook page that shows the couple enjoying their relationship and raising their three kids in Madisonville, Texas. They seem to cherish every moment together; their bond is evident in their photographs.

Mark Worked In Dental Medicine

Sam father Dr. Mark Leon Bennett was born in Houston on August 29, 1967 to Dr. Mac Leon Butch and Sandra Sue (Peters) Bennett. 

He graduated from Madisonville High School, Texas A & M University, and the University of Texas Dental Branch. Mark served as a Captain in the U.S. Air Force before starting his dental practice, where he worked alongside his father for many years.

As a dental specialist, Bennett’s area of expertise included preventing, diagnosing, and treating diseases and conditions associated with the mouth and overall dental health. 

However at 53, Mark passed away on June 8, 2021, in College Station, Texas, after a seven-year battle with early-onset Alzheimer’s.

Tragically, Sam lost his father to the disease shortly after the end of his junior season. By Sam’s senior year of high school, Mark had already lost the ability to recognize him and his older brothers.

It’s been challenging for Bennett to remember his father’s healthy days before he lost the ability to communicate. Bennett finds solace in videos that help him recall his father’s past. He holds on dearly to the last five words of advice his father gave him “Don’t wait to do something.” 

These words are tattooed on Bennett’s left forearm in his father’s handwriting, and he looks down at them every day.

Sam Bennet Mother

Sam Bennet mom Stacy Bennett is a language arts teacher at Madisonville Junior High School. 

In addition, her Facebook bio also highlights that she teaches 7th and 8th-grade ELA/Rdg, which refers to English Language Arts/Reading.

Furthermore, Stacy is an outdoor enthusiast who enjoys biking, hiking, and other outdoor activities. She is also a graduate of Madisonville High School and later went on to study at Sam Houston State University, which is located in Huntsville, Texas.

Stacy frequently shares pictures of her family on social media platforms. Many of these images showcase her children’s achievements in their respective careers, which indicates the strong bond and support within the family.

Sam Bennett Siblings

Sam Bennett family includes his two elder brothers Marcus Bennett and Jake Bennett. They were all born with a passion for sports, which was encouraged by their parents.

They attended Madisonville High School, where they all excelled in golf among other sports.

Mark, in particular, played an essential role in introducing his sons to golf. He taught them how to play on the nine-hole course in Madisonville, which ignited their passion for the sport.

As a family, they enjoyed playing golf and often spent their weekends on the golf course. Sam, Marcus, and Jake were often seen playing golf together in their early years of high school, and they continued to pursue the sport through their college years at Texas A&M.

Marcus Bennett

The eldest of the three siblings, Marcus is married to his high school girlfriend Chloe Bennett. 

Marcus and Chloe began dating while they were still in high school. The couple was together for several years before they decided to take their relationship to the next level. They got engaged and soon began planning their wedding.

On January 13, 2018, Marcus and Chloe exchanged their wedding vows in a beautiful ceremony their close friends and family attended. The wedding ceremony was held at a local venue in Madisonville, Texas, where the couple had grown up.

After their wedding, Marcus and Chloe continued to build their life together. They started a family and welcomed their son, Jett Leon Bennett, in October 2019.

Furthermore, Marcus shares pictures from his high school days and some moments with his younger brothers on his Instagram account with the handle @marcus_imtheman.

Jake Bennett

Jake Bennett is a 6th-grade science teacher at Willis. He resides in Huntsville Texas with wife Chelsie Breauna Bennett.

The couple recited their vows on 6th March 2021. Additionally, Jake and Chelsie are high school sweethearts, having met each other when they were 16 and 17 years old respectively.

Jake’s wife, Chelsie, works as a teacher at a school. Apart from their wedding, little information is available publicly about their personal lives. 

However, it is known that Jake is three years older than his younger brother, Sam, who was born on December 21, 1999, making him 23 years old.

Sam Bennett Childhood Story

Bennett was first introduced to golf at Oak Ridge Country Club. Despite the course’s unimpressive appearance, Bennett enjoyed the challenge and appreciated the freedom to develop his swing, without any formal training.

His swing may vary from day to day, but Bennett trusts his instincts and relies on his feel for the game.

Bennett’s athletic prowess was not limited to golf. In addition to winning a state championship in golf in 2016, he attended Madisonville High School, where he excelled in basketball, baseball, and tennis. 

Per Golf Monthly, he was recognized as an All-District shooting guard, a defensive standout on the baseball diamond, and a district champion on the tennis court.

After high school, Bennett continued his golf career at Texas A&M University, where he achieved great success. As a junior, he earned All-American honors and represented the American team at the 2021 Arnold Palmer Cup.

Bennett was named the SEC Player of the Year in his senior year. He was a finalist for the prestigious Fred Haskins Award, given to the most outstanding collegiate golfer in the United States.

In 2022, Bennett became the champion of the U.S. Amateur Championship, one of the most grueling and challenging tournaments in the golf world. 

Despite facing fierce competition from other highly ranked golfers, Bennett emerged victorious, cementing his status as one of the top amateur golfers in the world.

Sam Bennett Girlfriend

Sam Bennett girlfriend Harper Walding is a principle administrative specialist at Halliburton since July 2022. The couple began their relationship in 2021.

The couple met while studying at Texas A&M University from 2017-2021. Harper earned her RPTS, Tourism, and Travel Services Management degree in December 2021.

Their relationship was first publicly mentioned on Sam’s mother’s Facebook page in November 2021 when the couple was together at The Spirit Amateur International.

Later, Harper also posted a picture of herself and Sam at a golf tournament in February 2022, indicating their strong bond and support for each other.

In July 2022, Harper worked as a principal administrative specialist at Halliburton, a multinational oil and gas company, per her LinkedIn page. 

While there is limited information about their relationship, Sam and Harper seem to share a passion for golf and enjoy supporting each other in their personal and professional lives.
