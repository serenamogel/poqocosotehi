Collier Gwin San Francisco Art Dealer Sprays Homeless Woman

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Tuesday, February 13, 2024

Foster Gwin Gallery’s owner Collier Gwin received criticism for his behavior. Is he arrested? 

Collier Gwin is the owner of Foster Gwin Gallery, an art gallery. 

He is from Austin, Texas, USA, even though he currently resides in San Francisco, California.

He made headlines after one of his social media videos featuring him spraying a homeless woman with water went viral.

SocketSite claims that since opening in 1984, his art gallery has attracted A-list patrons like Schwarzenegger.

Due to the incident, the gallery’s owner Gwin has subsequently experienced a damaged front door and a flood of one-star Google reviews; the Company is currently listed as temporarily closed.

Collier Gwin was not arrested during the “potential assault” incident. The SFPD is still looking into the event.

A woman screams in terror as a man sprays her with water from a garden hose. He has refused to apologize while claiming to be the man in the video.

According to media accounts, Gwin has also defended his actions and claimed that the woman was insane and started tossing trash cans.

Gwin also claimed that despite numerous requests for the woman to move so that he could clean the streets, she chose to attack him instead.

He then allegedly sprayed her with water from the yard hose in revenge, according to the claims.

Before the event, Collier continued, he had permitted her to remain on the sidewalk for several days while attempting to contact the Police and other emergency services to seek her assistance.

Gwin wasn’t immediately accessible when DailyMail.com asked for a comment, but he eventually responded. Additionally, the Company’s social media accounts have been deleted.

What Did Collier Gwin Say Regarding His Viral Video?

Collier Gwin tried to justify his behavior by asserting that the woman was insane and throwing trash cans after finally admitting that he was the man in the video.

He said that days before the incident, he had let her remain on the sidewalk while attempting to contact the Police and other emergency services to try to assist her.

Have Other Business Owners Criticized Gwin’s Behavior? 

Yes, several other business owners have criticized Gwin’s behavior.
